Pricing

Base plan

The Base Plan is the best choice for small teams looking for a robust site management solution. It offers an extensive range of digital asset management tools for images, videos, PDFs, and more. Additionally, the Plan comes with versatile Content Management features to manage articles, projects, services, galleries, features and notes. All with access to website management tools for creating and managing your organization's website. Maximize your team's potential with the Base Plan and experience the best digital asset and content management solutions and is $800/mo.

Plus plan

The Plus Plan is the ideal solution for midsize organizations that need to manage their website, while also providing users with access to marketing or sales portals. It is packed with robust analytics to give you a complete picture of customer engagement and behavior. Plus, tools for managing content, monitoring performance, and tracking sales activity. Get the clear insights and data that can help you maximize returns and stay competitive and starts at $2,250/mo.

Premium plan

The Premium Plan is optimized for large organizations that want integrations into existing systems, desire to host KNVEY within their own cloud infrastructure and require unlimited KNVEY Sites, KNVEY Portals and KNVEY Guides. The Premium Plan has access to all features with KNVEY and has custom analytics to provide an even more complete picture of customer engagement and behavior. Workflow management and AI integrations make this plan ideal for organizations that are seeking enhanced productivity and starts at $5,500/mo.
